Welcome to Katie’s K9s

Katie’s K9s was founded in 2024 when Katie made the decision to leave the corporate world and follow her true passion to create a safe, loving, homely place for dogs to stay.

What started as a dream has become a thriving small business where dogs are welcomed as part of the family.

Our mission is simple: to give your dog the comfort, care and companionship they deserve whilst you’re away, it’s their holiday too after all!

Whether it’s for a day at doggy day care or a longer boarding stay, we provide a warm, relaxed environment where every dog can feel settled and at home.

We’re proud to be a high-standard licensed home boarder (Licence No: CE/HB232). Katie’s K9s is fully insured, DBS checked and trained in canine first aid, so you can have complete peace of mind knowing your furry family member is in safe hands.

Every dog is different, and we welcome them all – from the lively and playful to the calm and cuddly. We tailor our care to each dog’s needs and routine, making sure they feel comfortable, secure and loved throughout their stay.

At Katie’s K9s, our services include home dog boarding, doggy day care and drop-in visits. All designed to offer a cosy, caring, home-from-home experience for your beloved pets.

Because here, it’s not just about looking after dogs, it’s about giving them a family and a place where they feel truly at home.

We provide a safe, homely and caring environment for your dogs, giving you the reassurance that your fur babies are in the best hands when away from home.
