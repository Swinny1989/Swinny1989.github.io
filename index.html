Welcome to Katie’s K9s

Katie’s K9s was founded in 2024 after Katie chose to take the leap out of the corporate world and turn her passion for dogs into a thriving small business.

It is our mission to provide the highest quality of care, enrichment and stimulation to your beloved dog whilst in our care. Whether that’s for a mini adventure on a group walk, or joining our family and settling into their home from home for a long stay, we promise to always deliver the best care and experiences to your furriest family members.

We provide a safe, homely and caring environment for your dogs, giving you the reassurance that your fur babies are in the best hands when away from home. Katie’s K9s is fully insured, DBS checked and holds qualifications in canine first aid and Ofqual regulated level 3 canine care, behaviour and welfare.

At Katie’s K9s we welcome all different dog personalities, the lively, shy, energetic and those who just want to enjoy a cuddly snooze! We will cater to your preferences and your dog’s needs.

We offer a wide range of services including home boarding, doggy day care, group walks and drop ins. Please see our services page for more information.
